Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a complete bathroom remodel take?

A standard bathroom remodel typically takes 2-3 weeks from demolition to completion. Larger or more complex projects may require 4-6 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

What warranties and guarantees do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive 5-year workmanship warranty and lifetime warranties on most fixtures and materials. Our waterproofing system comes with a 25-year guarantee. All warranties are fully transferable if you sell your home.

What is your pricing structure and payment process?

Projects typically range from $15,000 to $50,000 depending on scope and materials. We offer flexible payment plans and accept major credit cards. A 25% deposit is required to begin work, with remaining payments tied to project milestones.

Are you licensed, bonded, and insured?

Yes, we maintain all required state licenses, carry $2M in liability insurance, and are fully bonded. All our contractors are certified and undergo regular training on the latest installation techniques.

Can you help with design and material selection?

Absolutely! Our design team offers free consultations to help you choose materials, colors, and fixtures that match your style and budget. We partner with leading suppliers to offer exclusive products and competitive pricing.
